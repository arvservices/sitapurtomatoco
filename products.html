Fresh Produce

Fresh Produce Varieties

Hand-picked, carefully graded, and packed to international standards. Our fresh vegetables are exported via refrigerated containers to preserve peak freshness.

Red Round Tomatoes

Our signature export variety. Firm, uniformly red, and packed with flavor. Ideal for retail markets, restaurants, and food service. Available in multiple size grades (S, M, L, XL) and packed in 5kg, 10kg, or 25kg cartons.

Size: 47-67mm Brix: 4.0-5.0° Shelf Life: 14-21 days Season: Year-round

Cherry Tomatoes

Sweet, bite-sized cherry tomatoes grown in controlled poly-house environments. Available in classic red, golden yellow, and mixed color packs. Perfect for premium retail, salad bars, and gourmet food industries.

Size: 15-30mm Brix: 7.0-9.0° Shelf Life: 10-14 days Packaging: 250g punnets

Roma / Plum Tomatoes

Elongated, meaty Roma tomatoes with low moisture content and rich, concentrated flavor. The preferred variety for sauce-making, canning, and paste production. Dense flesh with minimal seeds.

Size: 50-80mm length Brix: 4.5-5.5° Shelf Life: 15-20 days Use: Processing

Beefsteak Tomatoes

Large, juicy beefsteak tomatoes ideal for slicing, burgers, and sandwich applications. Rich in lycopene with a classic tomato taste. Sourced from select farms with optimal growing conditions.

Size: 80-120mm Weight: 200-400g each Shelf Life: 10-14 days Use: Retail/HoReCa

Pumpkin

Fresh, mature pumpkins sourced from trusted farms and selected for firm texture, bright color, and reliable cooking quality. Suitable for retail, wholesale, food service, and processing buyers.

Grade: Export Quality Packing: Mesh bags/cartons Shelf Life: 30-45 days Use: Retail/Processing

Lemon

Fresh lemons with a clean citrus aroma, balanced acidity, and consistent sizing. Carefully sorted for freshness and packed for domestic wholesale and export supply chains.

Size: Mixed grades Packing: 5kg/10kg cartons Shelf Life: 21-30 days Use: Retail/HoReCa

Cucumber

Crisp, fresh cucumbers graded for uniform shape, clean skin, and good hydration. Ideal for fresh markets, supermarkets, salad suppliers, hotels, and catering buyers.

Grade: A/B available Packing: Crates/cartons Shelf Life: 10-14 days Use: Fresh Market

Quality Assurance

Our Quality Standards

Every product leaving our facility undergoes rigorous multi-stage quality control — from pre-harvest field inspections to final export documentation.

  • Pre-harvest field inspections for pesticide residue compliance
  • Automated optical sorting and color grading machines
  • Cold room storage at 10-12°C with 85-90% relative humidity
  • Phytosanitary treatment and certificate generation
  • MRL (Maximum Residue Limit) testing per EU/US/Gulf standards
  • Complete traceability from farm to container
  • HACCP-compliant processing for all value-added products
