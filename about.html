Our Story

Two Decades of Growing Excellence

Sitapur Tomato Company was founded in 2005 by a visionary farmer who saw the immense potential in Sitapur's fertile alluvial soil and favorable climate for tomato cultivation. What started as a 10-acre farm supplying local mandis has grown into a 500+ acre agricultural enterprise exporting over 10,000 metric tons of premium tomatoes annually.

Our founder's philosophy was simple: grow the best tomatoes nature can offer and deliver them to the world with integrity. That ethos remains the cornerstone of everything we do today — from seed selection to international shipping.

Over the years, we've invested in modern farming technology, built state-of-the-art grading and packing facilities, and forged trade relationships with importers across 25+ countries. Yet, at our core, we remain a Sitapur company, deeply rooted in our community.

Our Journey

Milestones

2005

The Beginning

Founded as a 10-acre tomato farm in Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh, supplying fresh produce to local markets.

2010

First Export Shipment

Secured APEDA registration and shipped our first container of fresh tomatoes to the UAE market.

2014

Cold Chain Infrastructure

Invested in a modern 5,000 sq ft cold storage and grading facility to ensure year-round export capability.

2018

Global Expansion

Expanded to 15+ export destinations including Southeast Asia, Europe, and East Africa. Crossed 5,000 MT annual exports.

2022

Processing Unit Launch

Launched tomato processing facility for paste, puree, and dehydrated tomato products — adding value to exports.

2025

10,000 MT Milestone

Achieved 10,000+ MT annual export volume, serving 25+ countries with premium tomato products.
