Who We Are

Rooted in Sitapur, Reaching the World

Established in 2005, Sitapur Tomato Company began as a small family-owned farm in the fertile plains of Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh. Today, we are one of India's most trusted tomato exporters, supplying premium-grade fresh tomatoes and processed tomato products to markets across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Europe, and Africa.

Our commitment to quality starts at the soil. We work directly with over 200 local farmers, providing them with advanced agricultural training and fair trade practices to ensure every tomato meets international standards.

  • APEDA & FSSAI Certified Export House
  • Direct partnerships with 200+ local farmers
  • State-of-the-art cold chain infrastructure
  • GlobalG.A.P. compliant farming practices
