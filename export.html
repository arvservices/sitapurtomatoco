How It Works

Our Export Process

A streamlined 6-step process ensures your order moves efficiently from inquiry to delivery.

Inquiry & Quotation

Share your requirements — variety, quantity, packaging, and destination. We provide a detailed quotation within 24 hours.

Order Confirmation

Upon agreement on pricing and terms, we confirm the order with a proforma invoice and production schedule.

Harvesting & Grading

Tomatoes are harvested at optimal maturity, washed, graded by size/color, and quality-inspected at our facility.

Packing & Cold Storage

Products are packed per your specifications, pre-cooled, and stored in our cold rooms at 10-12°C until container loading.

Documentation & Customs

We handle all export documentation — commercial invoice, packing list, phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, BL/AWB.

Shipping & Delivery

Refrigerated containers are loaded and shipped via JNPT/Mundra/Chennai ports. Real-time tracking provided until delivery.
